About Hongxin Electronics

We are more than a distributor. We are your partner in power electronics design, providing authentic components and expert technical support.

Our Mission

To empower engineers and designers by providing reliable, high-performance Jianghai capacitors backed by unparalleled technical expertise and a commitment to customer success. We bridge the gap between a world-class manufacturer and the innovative companies building the future of technology.

Our History as an Authorized Jianghai Distributor

Founded in 2010, Hongxin Electronics quickly recognized the critical need for high-quality passive components in the rapidly growing power electronics market. We established a strategic partnership with Jianghai Capacitor, becoming one of their first-tier authorized distributors in the region. Over the past decade, we have grown alongside our customers, evolving from a simple component supplier to a full-service solutions provider. Our deep understanding of the Jianghai portfolio and our commitment to holding extensive inventory have made us a trusted partner for companies ranging from agile startups to publicly-listed industrial leaders.

Meet Our FAE Team

Our greatest asset is our team of Field Application Engineers (FAEs). Unlike traditional sales teams, our FAEs are experienced engineers with backgrounds in power supply design, motor control, and renewable energy systems. They understand your challenges because they've faced them. When you work with us, you gain access to this expertise, helping you with:

  • Component selection and optimization
  • Lifetime and performance simulations
  • Troubleshooting design issues
  • Alternative component recommendations

Our Commitment to Quality

In a market flooded with counterfeit and low-quality components, we guarantee 100% authentic, factory-traceable products. As an authorized distributor, our entire supply chain is secure and transparent. We provide full documentation and support, giving you the peace of mind to focus on innovation.
