Jianghai Announces New High Power Density Supercapacitor Series

Published: June 1, 2025

Our manufacturing partner, Jianghai, has officially announced the launch of their next-generation supercapacitor modules, the ECH series. This new series targets applications requiring high power density for peak power assist and short-term backup power, such as in smart meters, industrial robotics, and regenerative braking systems.

Key features of the new ECH series include:

  • Higher Energy Density: Up to 20% more energy stored in the same physical footprint compared to the previous generation.
  • Lower ESR: Reduced internal resistance allows for higher discharge power and improved efficiency.
  • Extended Operating Temperature Range: Certified for operation in more demanding thermal environments.

As an authorized distributor, Hongxin Electronics will have priority access to stock and samples of the new ECH series. We are excited to work with our customers to integrate this leading-edge technology into their next-generation products. Please contact our sales team for preliminary datasheets and sample requests.
