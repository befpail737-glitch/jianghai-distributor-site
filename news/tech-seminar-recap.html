Recap: Hongxin Electronics Hosts Technical Seminar on High-Frequency Power Design

Published: May 15, 2025

Last week, Hongxin Electronics was proud to host over 50 design engineers from leading companies in the renewable energy and industrial automation sectors for a technical seminar focused on "Passive Component Selection for High-Frequency Power Design."

Led by our Senior FAE, John Doe, the session dove deep into the challenges and opportunities presented by the industry's shift towards wide-bandgap semiconductors like SiC and GaN.

Key topics included:

  • The critical impact of ESR and ESL in DC-Link capacitors operating above 100kHz.
  • A comparative analysis of Film vs. Electrolytic capacitor technologies in high-ripple applications.
  • Thermal modeling techniques to accurately predict capacitor lifetime.
  • A preview of upcoming ultra-low inductance components from Jianghai.

The event concluded with a lively Q&A session and a hands-on look at product samples. We thank all who attended and look forward to our next event. For those who missed it, key presentation materials can be found in our Technical Support section.
