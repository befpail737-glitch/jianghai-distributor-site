Capacitor Solutions for New Energy Vehicles (NEVs)

The automotive industry is undergoing a massive transformation with the rise of New Energy Vehicles (NEVs). These vehicles demand electronic components that meet the highest standards of reliability, performance, and safety. As an authorized Jianghai distributor, we provide a wide range of AEC-Q200 qualified capacitors that are specifically designed for the harsh automotive environment. From the powertrain to the charging system, Jianghai capacitors provide the performance and reliability needed to power the next generation of electric and hybrid vehicles.

On-Board Chargers (OBCs)

The On-Board Charger is a critical component in any NEV, responsible for converting AC power from the grid to DC power to charge the battery. Our capacitor solutions for OBCs include:

  • High-Voltage DC-Link Film Capacitors: Jianghai's CBB131 series of film capacitors are ideal for the DC-Link in OBCs, offering high voltage ratings, low ESR, and excellent reliability.
  • X and Y Capacitors for EMI Filtering: We offer a range of AEC-Q200 qualified X and Y capacitors for EMI filtering, ensuring that the OBC meets all relevant safety and EMC standards.

DC/DC Converters

DC/DC converters are used in NEVs to convert the high voltage from the main battery to a lower voltage for powering the vehicle's accessories. We provide a range of high-reliability aluminum electrolytic and film capacitors for the input and output filtering of DC/DC converters.

Main Inverter

The main inverter is the heart of the NEV's powertrain, responsible for converting DC power from the battery to AC power to drive the electric motor. This is a very demanding application that requires high-power, low-ESR capacitors for the DC-Link. We offer a range of high-performance film capacitors from Jianghai that are specifically designed for this application.
