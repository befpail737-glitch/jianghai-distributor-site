Variable Frequency Drives (VFDs)

VFDs are critical for controlling motor speed in a wide range of industrial applications. The DC bus within a VFD requires large energy storage, which is typically handled by robust aluminum electrolytic capacitors. Our solutions for VFDs focus on:

  • High-Capacitance Screw Terminal Capacitors: Jianghai's CD 130 series offers very high capacitance in a rugged package, perfect for handling the large energy storage requirements of the DC bus.
  • High Ripple Current Snap-in Capacitors: For smaller drives, the CD 296 series provides excellent ripple current handling in a PCB-mountable form factor.
  • Reliable Snubber Film Capacitors: To protect the IGBTs from voltage spikes during switching, we recommend Jianghai's CBB81 series of film capacitors, which offer high dv/dt and low ESL.

Programmable Logic Controllers (PLCs)

PLCs are the brains of most automated systems, and their power supplies must be incredibly reliable. We offer a range of long-life, high-reliability capacitors for PLC power supplies, ensuring stable and uninterrupted operation.

Robotics

Robotic systems require compact, high-performance components. We provide a range of miniaturized aluminum electrolytic and film capacitors that are ideal for robotic arms, controllers, and power supplies, where space is at a premium but performance cannot be compromised.
