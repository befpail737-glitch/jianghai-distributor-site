Capacitor Solution for Industrial Variable Frequency Drives (VFDs)

Published: April 10, 2025 | Category: Solutions

1. Solution Introduction

Variable Frequency Drives (VFDs) are the workhorses of industrial automation, but they operate in electrically and thermally stressful environments. The DC Bus capacitors within a VFD are arguably the most critical components for reliability. They must absorb and deliver enormous ripple currents generated during motor operation. This solution utilizes Jianghai's most robust capacitors to build a VFD front-end that is built to last.

3. Core Advantages of this Solution

  • Maximum Ripple Current Handling: Employs large can screw terminal electrolytic capacitors designed specifically for the high RMS currents found in VFD DC link applications.
  • High Reliability: The components selected feature high vibration resistance and are designed for long life at elevated operating temperatures typical of an industrial control cabinet.
  • Switch Protection: Includes high dV/dt snubber film capacitors placed directly across the IGBT modules to suppress destructive voltage spikes during switching events.
  • Complete Solution: Covers all key capacitor needs from the main DC bus to the auxiliary power and control logic, all from a single trusted manufacturer.
