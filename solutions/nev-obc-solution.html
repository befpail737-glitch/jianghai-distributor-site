Capacitor Solution for New Energy Vehicle (NEV) On-Board Chargers & DC/DC

Published: April 15, 2025 | Category: Solutions

1. Solution Introduction

The automotive environment is one of the most challenging for electronic components. On-Board Chargers (OBCs) and DC/DC converters in electric vehicles demand components that can withstand extreme temperatures, constant vibration, and high electrical stress, all while meeting stringent safety and reliability standards. This solution leverages Jianghai's automotive-grade (AEC-Q200) capacitors to deliver a robust, reliable, and compact power conversion solution for EVs.

3. Core Advantages of this Solution

  • Automotive Grade Reliability: All recommended key components are qualified to the AEC-Q200 standard, ensuring they meet the rigorous demands for temperature, humidity, and vibration resistance.
  • High Power Density: Utilizes both miniaturized electrolytic capacitors and high-performance DC-Link film capacitors to achieve a compact design, which is critical for saving space and weight in an EV.
  • Enhanced Safety: Employs certified X and Y safety capacitors for EMI filtering to ensure the vehicle meets international safety and electromagnetic compatibility (EMC) standards.
  • Thermal Management: The chosen capacitors have excellent thermal characteristics, with low ESR to minimize self-heating, a crucial factor in sealed automotive power modules.
