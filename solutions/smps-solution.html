Capacitor Solution for High-Power Switch-Mode Power Supplies (SMPS)

Published: April 20, 2025 | Category: Solutions

1. Solution Introduction

In the world of data centers and telecommunications, efficiency and reliability are paramount. Power supplies achieving 80 PLUS Titanium ratings require every component to be optimized for minimal loss. The bulk storage capacitors in the Power Factor Correction (PFC) stage and the output filtering capacitors are key to meeting these stringent demands. This solution leverages Jianghai's high-performance capacitors to build SMPS units with maximum efficiency and power density.

3. Core Advantages of this Solution

  • Maximum Efficiency: Utilizes low-ESR electrolytic and film capacitors to minimize I²R losses, helping to achieve high efficiency ratings like 80 PLUS Platinum and Titanium.
  • High Power Density: Employs miniaturized, high-ripple current capacitors that allow for a more compact power supply design without sacrificing performance.
  • Long-Term Reliability: Relies on long-life 105°C capacitors to ensure the power supply meets the extended operational life required in server and telecom infrastructure.
