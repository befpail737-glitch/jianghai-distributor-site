Capacitor Solution for Energy Storage Systems (ESS)

Published: April 25, 2025 | Category: Solutions

1. Solution Introduction

Grid-scale Energy Storage Systems (ESS) are essential for stabilizing the power grid and enabling the growth of renewable energy. The Power Conversion System (PCS) within a Battery ESS (BESS) is the critical interface between the batteries and the grid. Its DC-Link capacitors must endure decades of service, handling continuous, high-power charge and discharge cycles. This solution combines the immense bulk capacitance of screw terminal capacitors with the reliability of film capacitors to create a PCS front-end built for maximum endurance.

2. Solution Block Diagram

Fig. 1: DC-Link capacitor bank within a BESS Power Conversion System.

3. Core Advantages of this Solution

  • Extreme Durability & Lifespan: A hybrid bank of long-life screw terminal and film capacitors provides a solution designed to match the 20+ year lifespan of the battery installation.
  • High Cycle Endurance: Film capacitors handle high-frequency switching ripple, while the large screw terminal capacitors provide the bulk energy storage, protecting both from excessive stress and extending their operational life.
  • Scalability: This modular approach allows for the creation of multi-megawatt capacitor banks by paralleling components to meet any power requirement.
  • Safety and Reliability: Utilizes proven, robust components designed for the demands of utility-scale power electronics.
