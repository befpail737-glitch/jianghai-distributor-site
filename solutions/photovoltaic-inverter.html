Capacitor Solution for High-Reliability Photovoltaic (PV) Inverters

Published: March 15, 2025 | Category: Solutions

1. Solution Introduction

The reliability of a photovoltaic (PV) inverter is paramount, as it is expected to operate continuously for up to 25 years in harsh outdoor environments. Capacitors, particularly in the DC-Link and auxiliary power stages, are critical components that directly impact the inverter's lifespan and efficiency. This solution leverages the high-performance portfolio of Jianghai to address these challenges, ensuring maximum energy yield and long-term stability.

3. Core Advantages of this Solution

  • Extended Lifespan: Utilizes film capacitors with self-healing properties for the critical DC-Link stage and 105°C long-life aluminum electrolytic capacitors for auxiliary power, matching the 25-year design life of solar panels.
  • High Efficiency: Jianghai's DC-Link film capacitors feature extremely low ESR (Equivalent Series Resistance) and ESL (Equivalent Series Inductance), minimizing power losses and improving the overall efficiency of the inverter.
  • Thermal Performance: Selected components are designed to operate reliably at high ambient temperatures, a common condition for inverters installed outdoors.
  • Optimized Cost-Performance: Provides a robust technical solution while maintaining cost-effectiveness, crucial for the competitive solar energy market.
