Jianghai Supercapacitors (EDLC)

As an authorized distributor for Jianghai, we provide cutting-edge supercapacitor (also known as EDLC or ultracapacitor) solutions. Jianghai supercapacitors offer extremely high power density, rapid charge/discharge capabilities, and an exceptionally long cycle life, making them a perfect fit for a wide range of modern electronic applications. From providing peak power assist in electric vehicles and industrial equipment to ensuring reliable short-term backup for critical systems, these components are designed for performance and longevity. Explore our range of single cells and multi-cell modules to find the ideal energy storage solution for your project.
