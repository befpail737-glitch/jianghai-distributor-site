Jianghai Aluminum Electrolytic Capacitors

As an official Jianghai distributor, we provide a comprehensive portfolio of Aluminum Electrolytic Capacitors renowned for their high reliability, long operational life, and excellent performance-to-cost ratio. These capacitors are the backbone of countless power electronics applications, from compact switch-mode power supplies to massive industrial inverters. Jianghai's offerings cover a wide voltage and capacitance range, with key series like the CD snap-in capacitors for PCB mounting and the robust screw terminal series for high-power DC-Link applications. Whether you need high ripple current capability, extended temperature range, or a compact footprint, our expert team can help you with your Jianghai capacitor selection to ensure optimal performance and longevity for your project.

How to Select the Right Jianghai Capacitor for Your Inverter Application

Snap-in Capacitors

Ideal for PCB mounting in power supplies, inverters, and drives. High ripple current and long life.

Screw Terminal Capacitors

Designed for high-power applications requiring robust connections and maximum capacitance/voltage.
